Our Story

We started this service because we kept seeing the same problem: great solar installers losing time — and sometimes jobs — because the DNO application process was slow, confusing, and inconsistent.

Different DNOs. Different portals. Different rules. And if you got anything wrong, the whole thing bounced back.

We set up a dedicated service to take that off your plate. Every application we submit is checked for accuracy before it goes in. We track it through the DNO's system, chase when needed, and get you the export number as soon as it's issued.

We work with trade solar installers and homeowners who are designing their own systems. Whatever your situation, everything we do is built around getting your application right first time.

"We used to spend half a day on every DNO solar application. Now we just send the job details across and get the export number back within a couple of days."

How We Work

Every application goes through the same rigorous process.

1

Accuracy check

Every application is reviewed for completeness and accuracy before submission. We don't send anything we're not confident in.

2

Tracked through the DNO

Once submitted, we monitor progress and chase the DNO if needed. You don't have to follow up or wonder what's happening.

3

Export number delivered

As soon as the export number is issued, we forward it to you. No delays, no chasing us for updates.

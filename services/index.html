Everything You Need to Get Grid Connected — Done For You

Submitting a DNO application sounds simple. In reality, it means navigating different portals for different network operators, knowing when a G98 notification is enough and when a full G99 application is required, providing the correct technical specifications, and then waiting — and chasing. We handle all of it.

What We Do

G98 Notifications

Up to 3.68kW/phase

For systems up to 3.68 kW per phase — the standard for single-phase domestic installs.

  • We complete and submit the G98 notification to your DNO
  • Correct for single-phase domestic installs
  • Turnaround: typically 24–48 hours
  • Confirmation forwarded directly to you

G99 Applications

Over 3.68kW/phase

Full DNO application for larger or commercial solar systems requiring formal approval.

  • We prepare technical documentation and manage the submission
  • Direct liaison with the DNO engineering team on your behalf
  • Includes follow-up and chasing until approved
  • Commercial and large residential systems covered

Export Number Retrieval

Required for Smart Export Guarantee registration — we get it and pass it straight to you. Some DNOs issue an acceptance email instead of export numbers, which the supplier uses to request an export MPAN.

  • We obtain your export number from the DNO after approval
  • Required for Smart Export Guarantee registration
  • Forwarded directly to you or your customer

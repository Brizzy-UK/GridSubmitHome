Common Questions

What's the difference between G98 and G99?

G98 covers systems up to 3.68 kW per phase — these are notified to the DNO rather than applied for. G99 applies to larger systems and requires a formal application and approval before installation.

How long does a DNO application take?

G98 notifications are typically confirmed within 24–48 hours. G99 applications vary by DNO but usually take 10–30 working days. We chase on your behalf throughout.

Do you work with all DNOs?

Yes — we cover all major UK Distribution Network Operators including National Grid, UKPN, SSEN, SP Energy, ENW, and Northern Powergrid.

Can I submit jobs in bulk?

Yes. We regularly handle bulk submissions for installers running multiple jobs at once. Contact us to discuss volume pricing.

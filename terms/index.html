What we do

GridSubmit prepares and submits DNO applications (G98 notifications and G99 applications) to Distribution Network Operators on behalf of solar installers and homeowners in the United Kingdom. We also retrieve and forward export MPAN numbers once issued by the DNO.

We act as an agent submitting applications on your behalf. We do not make decisions on behalf of the DNO and cannot guarantee the outcome or timeline of any application, as these are determined solely by the relevant DNO.
